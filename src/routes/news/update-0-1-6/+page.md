## 0.1.6 — Feb 24, 2026

### New

**Kojaku (Quincy Bow)**  
Crafted at the Blacksmith's Anvil. Uses MP instead of arrows. Two abilities:

- **Q — Ōkami Ya (大神矢):** A single strong arrow that deals great damage in one hit. Must be fully charged to shoot.
- **E — Default Volley:** Consumes only 50% of Signature Energy.

**Four Point Quincy Cross** (off-hand) — Craftable at Workbench, adds 50 MP.

**Quincy weapon tiers** are now in place.

**Hollow Essence** added.

**"A Guide to Find Hollows" Scroll** — read the item description in-game.

### Changes

- Fixed Bare Shoulder Shinigami Robes
- Added SFX to Skull Crusher (Q — Kenpachi Zanpakuto)
- Slight damage tweaks for Kenpachi Zanpakuto Spin Charged Attack
- Shunpo VFX updated to differentiate it from Hirenkyaku
- **Concentrated Life Essence** craft now available at Soul Imprinting Altar
- **Reiryoku Crystal** craft now available at Soul Imprinting Altar
- Reiryoku Crystal crafting recipe changed
- Hollow-type enemy drops changed
- Hollow behaviour adjusted
- Wanderer Trader spawn reduced
- Hollow spawn adjusted
- Custom drop particle added for Bankai-tier weapons
