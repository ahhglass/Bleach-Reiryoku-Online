## 0.1.5 — Feb 16, 2026

### New

**Kenpachi's Zanpakuto** — Sealed Nozarashi is now in the mod.

**Six Hollow-type mobs:** Crawler Hollow, Scarak Defender Hollow, Scarak Louse Hollow, Scarak Fighter Hollow, Ghoul Hollow, and one more type to discover in-game.

**Shinigami Robes — Bare Shoulders** — New robe variant for more mobility and style.

### Changes

- **Crafting recipes** for most Zanpakuto updated: they now require **Asauchi** as a crafting ingredient.
- **Asauchi** no longer has durability and can be used as a crafting material.
- **Wabisuke** durability increased from 75 to 300.
- **Merchant spawn rate** reduced.

Thanks everyone for your reports and suggestions. We will keep working on the mod and adding new features. Please report any bugs in the Discord.
