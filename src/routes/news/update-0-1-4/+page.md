## 0.1.4 — Feb 8, 2026

### New

- **Wabisuke** — Curved blade with weight-doubling curse on parries.
- **Shinigami Captain Robes** — Captain-tier uniform.
- **Wandering Shinigami** — Merchant NPC.
- **Kan** — Soul Society currency for trading.
- **Basic Medical Pill** & **Kaido-Infused Pill** — Healing items.
- **Custom Slash Hit Effect** for all weapons.
- **Craftable Gotei 13 Banners** — Decorate with squad insignias.

### Changes

- Small model change to Shikai Zangetsu
- Reduced Tensa Zangetsu delay between attacks
- Tensa Zangetsu total combo damage set to 137
- Tensa Zangetsu now has custom trail on all combo hits
- Shinigami Robes textures replaced
- Trail updated for Shikai Zangetsu
- **Fixed:** Hakuren (Charged) no longer stops player movement when launching
- **Fixed:** Player can no longer move during swing animation on Hakuren (Signature)
